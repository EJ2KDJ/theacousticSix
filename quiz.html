Roberto "Bob" Ong

The Nostalgic Storyteller & Everyman Student

Roberto “Bob” Ong serves as the narrator and protagonist of the story. He is a relatable, observant, and humorous student who looks back at his school years with a mix of fondness and regret. His journey is filled with childhood struggles, teenage misadventures, and the realities of adulthood, making him a mirror of every Filipino student's experience.

Traits: Thoughtful, sentimental, self-deprecating, witty
Biggest Challenge: Navigating school life, discovering his purpose

Memorable Moments:

  • His struggles with reading and writing in elementary
  • Developing a crush on Special Someone
  • Embarrassing and funny moments in school
  • Reflecting on his past as an adult

Special Someone

The Mysterious Crush

She represents Bob Ong’s first love and ultimate "what if?". Special Someone is a classmate he deeply admires but never has the courage to pursue seriously. She is kind, smart, and seemingly out of reach—symbolizing the unfulfilled high school romance that many can relate to.

Traits: Sweet, intelligent, reserved
Biggest Challenge: Staying unaware of Bob Ong’s feelings

Memorable Moments:

  • Bob Ong secretly crushing on her during high school
  • Their awkward but meaningful interactions

Ulo

The Loyal & Hilarious Best Friend

Ulo is Bob Ong’s sidekick, providing comic relief throughout the story. He is loud, funny, and carefree—always joking around but also supporting Bob Ong through tough times. His humor helps balance out the stressful and emotional moments of school life.

Traits: Goofy, easygoing, fiercely loyal Biggest Challenge:
Balancing fun and responsibility

Memorable Moments:

  • Pulling pranks in class and getting in trouble
  • Giving funny advice about school and life

Portia

The Sweet & Friendly Girl Next Door

Portia is another notable female character who plays a part in Bob Ong’s school journey. She is friendly, approachable, and sometimes caught in the middle of the high school drama.

Traits: Kind, approachable, observant

Memorable Moments:

  • Being part of Bob Ong’s friend circle and school life
